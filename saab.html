Website is highly under construction!

Saab Simulator (working title: Saabulator) is a game under development.
The game is currently in alpha stage. The goal is to have a game in which you can drive at least Saab 900 and Saab 9000, repair them, buy parts for them and buy scrap cars and sell their parts and get profit.
